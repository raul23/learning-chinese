Asking the phone number
=======================
| Q: Nǐ de diànhuà hàomǎ shì shénme? (你的电话号码是什么?)

  - What's your phone number?
| A: Wǒ de diànhuà hàomǎ shì ... (我的电话号码是 ...) 

  - My phone number is ...
