Questions and answers
=====================
`:information_source:`

   - shéi de (谁的): whose
   - zhè (这): this

|

**Questions:**

| **Shéi de** shū (谁的书)?

   Whose book?
| **Shéi de** bēizi (谁的杯子)?

   Whose cup?
| **Zhè** shì **shéi de** qiánbāo? (这是谁的钱包)?

   Whose wallet is this? (lit. This is whose wallet?)

|

**Answers:**

| Lìlì **de** shū (莉莉的书).

   Lily's book.
| Ānnà **de** bēizi (安娜的杯子).

   Anna's cup.
| **Zhè** shì **wǒ de** qiánbāo (这是我的钱包).

   This is my wallet.
