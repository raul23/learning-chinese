Hello! (Nǐ hǎo! 你好!)
=====================
| Nǐ hǎo (你好)!
   
   Hello!

| Nǐ\ **men** hǎo (你们好)!

   Hello to **all** of you!

|

How are you? (Nǐ hǎo ma? 你好吗?)
================================
| Q: Nǐ hǎo **ma** (你好吗)?

   How are you?
| A: Wǒ hěn hǎo (我很好).

   I am very well.

|

| Q: Nǐ ne (你呢)?

   What about you?
   
| A: Wǒ **yě** hěn hǎo (我也很好).

   I am also very well.
