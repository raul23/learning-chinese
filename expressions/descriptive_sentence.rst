==============================
Descriptive sentence: hěn (很)
==============================
`:information_source:`

   - **hěn (很)**: very
   - No verb shì when using hěn

**Descriptive sentence:** Subject + **hěn** + adj

|

| Wǒ **hěn** hǎo (我很好).

   I am (very) good.
| Nǐmen **hěn** cōngmíng (你们很聪明).

   You are smart.
