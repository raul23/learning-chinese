Questions and answers
=====================
`:information_source:`

   - nà (那): that
   - zhè (这): this

|

**Questions:**

| **Nà** shì shénme (那是什么)?

   What is that?

| **Zhè** shì shénme (这是什么)?

   What is this?

|

**Answers:**

| **Nà** shì yī běn shū (那是一本书). 

   That is a book.
   
| **Zhè** shì yīgè píngguǒ (这是一个苹果).

   This is an apple.
