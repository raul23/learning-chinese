====================================
Negative sentence with "not" (bù 不)
====================================
.. contents:: **Contents**
   :depth: 3
   :local:
   :backlinks: top

|

`:information_source:`

   - **bù (不)**: not (adv)
   - Pronoun + to be + not + adj: Subject + bù + adj
   - No verb shì!

|

| Wǒ **bù** máng (我不忙).

   I am not busy. (lit. I not busy)
   
| Tā **bù** piàoliang (她不漂亮).

   She is not beautiful. (lit. She not beautiful)

|
